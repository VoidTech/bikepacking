Train with bikes (Stockholm → Hamburg): Book SJ/DB international. Bikes need reservation, usually €10–30/leg. Book early — bike spaces sell out.

Hamburg → Munich: ICE trains allow bikes (booked separately). ~6h. Alternatively overnight train.

Munich → Alps biking: The R1 Bavarian cycle route follows the Isar valley south. Mostly flat to Mittenwald, then climbing begins.

Brenner Pass (1374m): Paved road, manageable gradient. Historic route into South Tyrol, Italy. Great descent to Bolzano.

Return by train: Innsbruck → Salzburg → Munich → Hamburg → Stockholm. ÖBB Nightjet (overnight) saves a hotel and time.

Gear: Full bikepacking bags (frame, handlebar, seat). No panniers for train travel. Budget ~€60–100/day incl. accommodation.

Best time: June–September. July/August ideal for high mountain passes. Book trains 2–3 months ahead.
